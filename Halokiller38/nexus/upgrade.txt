[Upgrading a copy of Nexus]

o Delete the data folder from this archive (within garrysmod/) so that you do not overwrite your existing MySQL configuration.
o Extract the files in this archive to just outside of your garrysmod/ directory and overwrite any existing files.
o When you overwrite existing files, make sure to always overwrite everything, including external modules.
